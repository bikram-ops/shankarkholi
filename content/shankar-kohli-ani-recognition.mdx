## Industry Recognition

In India’s rapidly evolving luxury real estate landscape, Shankar Kohli has emerged as one of the most promising business leaders under 40.

His inclusion in ANI’s feature highlights a growing influence in how high-net-worth individuals approach property investments.

---

## Strategic Positioning in Luxury Real Estate

As the founder of Mark Real Estate, his focus has been sharply defined — premium residential investments above ₹5 crore in Gurugram.

This niche positioning has enabled a deeper understanding of luxury buyer behaviour and market cycles.

---

## Experience & Market Access

Since 2013, he has worked closely with leading developers including DLF, M3M India, Emaar Properties, Godrej Properties, and Sobha Realty.

This experience translates into:

- Early identification of high-potential projects  
- Access to premium and limited inventory  
- Strong developer-level relationships  

---

## Advisory Approach

What differentiates the model is a complete, end-to-end advisory structure:

- Identifying high-return opportunities  
- Negotiating optimal deal terms  
- Managing documentation and compliance  
- Assisting with financial structuring  
- Facilitating home loans  
- Supporting post-purchase portfolio management  

This creates a seamless experience for HNI and NRI investors.

## Focus on Branded Residences

A key area of specialization is branded luxury residences — a rapidly growing segment in India.

The portfolio includes developments such as:

- Westin Residences  
- Elie Saab Branded Residences  
- Armani Casa by Signature Global  

These assets combine lifestyle value with long-term capital appreciation.

## Investment Philosophy

The core strategy remains consistent:

- Focus on premium micro-markets  
- Enter early in the demand cycle
